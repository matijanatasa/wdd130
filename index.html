Overview

Purpose

During your days at work things can get heavy at times, right? Or do your clients get bored? What if you had a coworker, assistant or some other employee who is also a professional singer and would very gladly sing a song for you or your clients while doing their job whenever asked? I am a professional singer who happens to learn fast pretty much any job. I can improve your days and your business productivity by lightening up your mood or the mood of your clients so they will seek your services more often. On my site one can insert the job I’m asked to do and the site will show a list of songs that one can select for me to sing while working, otherwise one can let the site choose for them or one can send me a message and I will contact them back.

Audience

My intended audience is surely employers of any age, I guess they need to be of legal age. The fun fact is that it is intended for employers of pretty much all kinds, because I am a fast lerner, precise and willing to work passionately as I love learning new things and of course, singing. I bring with me a lot of value, so I'd look for employers with a righteous mind and full of optimism. Optimism is however optional because I can bring that with me.

About Me

My name is Matija Nataša and I’ve been singing since I was six in choirs and everywhere since I were a baby. Many times during my work hours I would start singing and things went smoother. That’s how I thought of making it officially so, that I should be a singing employee! I love learning new things and skills and that’s why I’m a fast learner. I’ve been a singer, a model, an interpreter, a translator, a teacher, an au pair, a shop assistant, a waitress, a hostess and I’ve acquired many skills from courses and life in general.
